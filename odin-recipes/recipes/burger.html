Burger Recipe

Description

The recipe involves needing the following ingrediants and steps:

Ingrediants:

  • Bread
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoe
  • Cheese
  • Beef
  • Sauce

Steps:

  1. Cook the beef
  2. Put beef on a bread side
  3. Put cheese on top of beef
  4. Put tomatoe on top of cheese
  5. Put lettuce on top of tomatoe
  6. Put last bun on top
